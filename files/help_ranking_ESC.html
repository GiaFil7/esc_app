Help with Rankings

Show selection

Use the dropdown menu to see your ranking for each Semi-Final and the Grand Final. However, you can only reorder the entries when viewing the full ranking.

Importing

Type your ranking as a comma-separated list using 2-letter country codes.

Exporting

Either copy the generated text or click the button next to the field to copy to clipboard. Make sure to click "Save" beforehand.

Saving

Clicking the save button stores your current ranking to the app's memory.
